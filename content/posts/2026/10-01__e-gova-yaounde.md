The African e-Governance Summit (e-Gov’A 2026) will take place in Yaoundé, Cameroon. Held under the theme “Artificial Intelligence and e-Governance: Building Efficient Public Services in a Cashless and Paperless Africa,” the event aims to bring together key stakeholders to accelerate the transition to more efficient digital public services across the continent.
